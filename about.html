About

I'm a computational biologist working at the intersection of computer science and biomedical data science. I design reproducible pipelines that turn large-scale genomic, transcriptomic, and epigenomic data into biological insight — spanning single-cell and spatial transcriptomics, chromatin profiling, and statistical genetics.

I care about making multi-omics analysis efficient, portable, and easy for collaborators to rerun and trust. Much of my work centers on building HPC- and cloud-based Nextflow pipelines and integrating diverse data types to uncover mechanisms of disease and evaluate treatment response.
