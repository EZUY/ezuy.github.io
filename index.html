About

I'm a computational biologist working at the intersection of computer science and biomedical data science. I design reproducible pipelines that turn large-scale genomic, transcriptomic, and epigenomic data into biological insight — spanning single-cell and spatial transcriptomics, chromatin profiling, and statistical genetics. I care about making multi-omics analysis efficient, portable, and easy for collaborators to rerun and trust.

Research interests

  • Single-cell & spatial transcriptomics
  • Chromatin profiling & epigenomics
  • Statistical & population genetics
  • Multi-omics integration
  • Reproducible workflows (Nextflow)
  • Gene therapy & tumor microenvironment
