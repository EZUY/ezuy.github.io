About

I'm a computational biologist with a background spanning computer science and biomedical data science. I collaborate with principal investigators and cross-disciplinary teams to support high-impact biomedical research — from tumor microenvironment studies and gene therapy development to chromatin profiling and complex-trait genetics.

My work centers on designing HPC- and cloud-based Nextflow pipelines that make large multi-omics analyses efficient and reproducible, and on integrating transcriptomic, epigenomic, proteomic, and genetic data to uncover mechanisms of disease, evaluate treatment response, and surface novel biomarkers and drug targets. I've co-authored peer-reviewed work in Cell, The Journal of Clinical Investigation, Nature Communications, Nature Neuroscience, and Current Biology.
